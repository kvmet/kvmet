The beadstead family of fonts can be found at https://bjh21.me.uk/bedstead/

They are included in this site under Creative Commons "CC0" as declared in the source repository.
To the best of my knowledge, the original font designs are considered public domain and the 
people working to reverse-engineer them have also claimed no additional rights. Consider 
supporting the creators by checking out the great and detailed work available on their site(s).
